About me

Xingke Yang is an Embedded Software Engineer in the smart meter solutions department at Hi-Trend Technology (Shanghai) Co., Ltd. Before that,he obtained a B.Eng. degree from Southwest Jiaotong University in June 2022.

He completed several research projects on intelligent devices under the supervision of Prof. Chaogen Jiang during college and achieved two National First Prizes in national class "A" competitions. His research experiences are related to building electromechanical control systems using embedded microcontrollers, so his research interests lie in Embedded Systems, Computer Architecture, Robot Control Systems, Mechatronics, Internet of Things and other related fields.
